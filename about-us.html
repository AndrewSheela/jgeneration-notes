About JGeneration Notes

Empowering worshippers, musicians, and believers to praise God through music with accuracy and devotion.

Our Mission & Faith

"Let everything that has breath praise the Lord." At JGeneration Notes, we believe that music is a powerful vessel for worship. Our heart is to serve Christian believers, worship leaders, and music students by providing an accessible, growing library of keyboard notes and chords for Tamil, Telugu, and multi-lingual Christian songs. Whether you are learning to play your first keyboard chord or leading your congregation in powerful worship, we are here to support your musical journey every step of the way.

How We Help You Grow

🎹 Keyboard Learning

Step-by-step notes and chords designed to help you master worship songs easily.

🎤 Singing & Worship

Accurate keys and tracks to help vocalists stay in sync and lead with confidence.
